Sloth

Sloths have a four-part stomach that very slowly digests the tough leaves they eat, it can sometimes take up to a month for them to digest a meal. Digesting this diet means a sloth has very little energy left to move around making it one of the slowest moving animals in the world.

  • Scientific name: Folivora
  • Average length: 58 to 68 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 20 to 30 years
  • Habits: spend almost all of their lives in trees

No creature in the animal kingdom is as chill as the tree-dwelling sloth. They spend their days hanging from branches, munching on leaves, sleeping and even going for the occasional swim. What's not to love?
